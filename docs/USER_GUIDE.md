## What is Keepswell?

Keepswell is a memory-keeping app that helps people gather the stories,
photos, and small everyday moments that make up a life. You can think of
it as a private group journal you share with the people who matter most —
without the noise of social media.

You can use Keepswell on its own, or invite family and friends to
contribute alongside you. Everyone responds when it suits them — by
text message, in the iOS app, or on the website — and their memories
appear together in one beautifully organized memory book.

We built Keepswell because so much of what matters gets lost in group
chats, camera rolls, and folders no one ever opens again. Keepswell is
the gentle place those moments can live, all together, and stay easy to
find when you want them.

## Who it's for

Keepswell is for anyone who wants to slow down and remember. Common
ways people are using it today:

- **Families.** Parents, kids, grandparents — invite everyone and watch
  a shared family story grow week by week.
- **New parents.** Capture the first smile, the first steps, the funny
  things your kids say — all the stuff you swear you'll remember but
  won't.
- **Couples.** Date nights, inside jokes, the trip you took on a whim.
  A private journal just for the two of you.
- **Travelers.** One memory book per trip. Drop photos and notes as
  you go, print or share when you're home.
- **Milestones.** Weddings, graduations, retirements, a year of
  recovery — invite everyone you love to share what they remember.
- **People remembering someone.** Gather stories from everyone who knew
  them. A living tribute you can revisit, add to, and share.

## How it works at a glance

The whole experience is built around three simple ideas: a **journal**
to hold the memories, **prompts** that gently invite people to share,
and a **memory book** that turns all of it into something beautiful to
look back on.

1. **Create a journal.** Pick a template (Family, Friends, Romantic,
   Vacation, Celebration, or Custom), give it a title, and decide how
   often you'd like prompts to arrive.
2. **Invite the people you want to share with.** Send invites by text
   or share a join link. Contributors don't need an app — they can
   respond by SMS if that's easier.
3. **Memories arrive on their own schedule.** A short prompt goes out
   on the cadence you choose. Anyone can respond with a story, a
   photo, or both — whenever they like.
4. **Read, react, and remember.** Open your memory book anytime to
   read what's been shared, react to favorites, and watch your family
   story take shape.

## Core concepts

### Journals & templates

A **journal** is one collection of memories. Most people have a few — a
family journal, a journal for each of their kids, a vacation journal, a
journal for a big upcoming milestone. Each one has its own prompts, its
own contributors, and its own memory book.

When you create a journal you start from a **template**. Templates set
the tone, suggest prompts, and shape what the finished memory book
looks like:

| Template | What it's for |
|---|---|
| **Family** | Warm, multi-generational prompts about traditions, milestones, and everyday life. |
| **Friends** | Playful, story-driven prompts perfect for a group you've been close to for years. |
| **Romantic** | Intimate prompts for couples about shared moments, gratitude, and the small things. |
| **Vacation** | Short, in-the-moment prompts designed to be answered on the go, so a trip doesn't fade. |
| **Celebration** | Built for milestones like weddings, retirements, and graduations, with prompts that invite guests to share their favorite memories. |
| **Custom** | Start blank and write all of your own prompts. Best when you have a very specific use in mind. |

You can change templates, edit any prompt, or add your own custom
prompts at any time. Templates are a starting point, not a cage.

### Writing prompts

Prompts are the quiet engine of Keepswell. Each one is a short question
or invitation — _"What's a small thing that made you smile this week?"_
— designed to make it easy to write something honest in under a
minute.

When you create a journal, Keepswell automatically generates a queue of
upcoming prompts from the template you picked. You're in full control
of that queue:

- **Preview** the next several prompts that will be sent and see
  exactly what's coming.
- **Edit** the wording on any upcoming prompt so it sounds more like
  you.
- **Cancel** a prompt you don't want to send.
- **Add** your own custom prompts that matter to your family or
  moment.
- **Turn prompts off entirely** if you'd rather use Keepswell as a
  journal you write in on your own schedule.

Prompts arrive on the cadence you choose — daily, weekly, biweekly, or
monthly — at a time and day-of-week of your choosing, in your local
timezone.

### Memories (entries)

A **memory** (or "entry") is one thing someone shares with the
journal. It can be a written story, one or more photos, or both.
There's no minimum, no formatting to fuss with, no word count to hit.
Anything you'd want to remember belongs.

When you respond to a prompt, your memory is automatically attached to
it. You can also add memories without a prompt anytime you have
something you want to capture.

Other people in the journal can **comment** on memories and add
**reactions** — small acknowledgments that say "I read this, I loved
it" without turning the journal into another social feed.

### Contributors (participants)

A journal is more meaningful when other people are in it with you.
**Contributors** can read every memory in the journal, add their own,
comment, and react. Each contributor gets their own profile in the
journal so memories stay credited to whoever shared them.

You can invite people three ways:

1. **By text message.** Enter their phone number and they'll receive a
   friendly invite they can respond to right from their phone.
2. **By join link or QR code.** Share a link (or a code) that opens a
   simple sign-up form. Great for events.
3. **From the iOS app.** Invite people directly from a journal's
   settings screen.

For each contributor, you decide how they receive prompts:

- **SMS** — text-only. They never need to install an app.
- **In-app** — push notification + in-app prompt feed. Best for
  contributors using the Keepswell mobile app.
- **Both** — SMS first, also surfaces in the in-app feed.

Owners can approve, pause, or remove contributors at any time from the
journal's settings.

### The Memory Book

Everything you and your contributors share automatically flows into a
**memory book** for each journal — a beautiful, readable view of every
memory in chronological order, themed to match the journal type. Family
books feel warm; vacation books feel breezy; celebration books feel
formal and timeless.

The memory book is meant to be read. It's not a feed. You can open it
any time on the web or in the iOS app and lose an hour flipping through
what your family has shared.

> The most common thing people tell us: they didn't realize how much
> had happened until they sat down and read it all in one place.

## Using the website

The Keepswell website at [keepswell.com](https://keepswell.com) is the
full experience — and the best place to manage journals, invite
contributors, browse the memory book on a big screen, and adjust
settings.

### Signing up

Click **Get Started** in the header. You can create an account with
your email and a password, or sign in with Google. We'll ask you to
agree to our Terms of Service and Privacy Policy when you sign up —
that's all the legal we need.

### Your dashboard

After signing in you land on your **Dashboard**, which shows every
journal you've created. Click one to open it; click **New Journal** to
start another.

### Creating a journal

On **Create Journal** you'll:

1. Pick a template.
2. Give the journal a title and an optional description.
3. Choose whether to include yourself as a contributor (and add your
   phone number if you'd like prompts by text).
4. Decide whether to turn on auto-generated prompts. We recommend
   leaving this on — you can always edit them later.

### The journal page

Each journal has its own page where you can:

- Browse every memory chronologically.
- Add a new memory yourself.
- See and manage contributors.
- Open the full Memory Book view.
- Open settings to edit prompts, schedule, cover image, sharing, and
  notifications.

### Settings you can adjust

- **Cover image** — pick from our curated set, paste an image URL, or
  upload your own.
- **Schedule** — frequency, day of week, time, and timezone for
  prompts.
- **Upcoming prompts** — preview, edit, cancel, or add custom prompts
  to the queue.
- **Prompt library** — reorder the pool of prompts the journal can
  pull from over time.
- **Notifications** — choose which events (new memories, comments,
  reactions, joins) you want to be notified about.
- **Sharing** — generate a public link so people can view the memory
  book without signing in.

## Using the iOS app

The Keepswell iOS app gives you the same journals and memories,
designed for capturing things on the go. It's a great fit for quick
photos and short voice-of-the-moment entries.

### Getting the app

Search for **Keepswell** on the App Store and download it. Sign in
with the same account you use on the website — your journals,
contributors, and memories all sync.

### Tabs

The app has three tabs at the bottom:

- **Journals** — every journal you own. Tap one to open it.
- **Prompts** — writing prompts waiting for you across all your
  journals. Tap _Respond_ on one to compose a memory; tap _Dismiss_ to
  clear it.
- **Settings** — your profile, subscription, and account.

### Capturing a memory

From any journal tap **+** to add a memory. You can type freely,
attach photos from your camera roll, or take a new photo right from
the app. There's no save button — your memory goes live when you tap
**Share**.

### Push notifications

With your permission, the app sends a gentle push when:

- A contributor adds a new memory.
- Someone comments on or reacts to a memory in your journal.
- A new contributor joins a journal you own.
- A new writing prompt is ready for you.

You can turn each of these on or off **per journal** in journal
settings → Notifications.

### The Memory Book on iOS

Each journal has its own beautifully themed memory book view inside
the app — perfect for handing your phone to a relative and saying
_"look at all this."_

## Contributing by text (SMS)

Not everyone wants another app on their phone, and Keepswell honors
that. Anyone you invite can fully participate in a journal using
nothing but text messages.

### How invites work

When you invite someone by phone number, they get a friendly text from
Keepswell letting them know who invited them and which journal they've
been added to.

- To **opt in**, they reply **YES**.
- To **opt out** at any time, they reply **STOP**.
- For **help**, they reply **HELP**.

### Receiving prompts by text

When a prompt is scheduled, contributors who chose SMS receive the
prompt as a regular text message. They can reply with:

- **Text** — turns into a written memory.
- **A photo** — attached to the memory.
- **Both** — even better.

Their reply automatically becomes a memory attached to that prompt.

### SMS terms

Message and data rates may apply. Message frequency varies based on
the journal's schedule. Your mobile information is never sold or
shared with third parties for promotional purposes. See the full SMS
terms at [keepswell.com/sms-consent](https://keepswell.com/sms-consent).

### Why SMS matters

The SMS option is what makes Keepswell different from "another app to
download." A grandparent who doesn't want to install anything new can
still share the story of how they met your grandmother — by text — and
it lands in your family memory book alongside everyone else's.

## Notifications & privacy

### You're in control of notifications

For each journal, you can independently turn on or off notifications
for:

- New memories
- New comments
- New reactions
- New contributors joining

Open the journal's settings and find the **Notifications** section.

### Your journals are private by default

Only you and the contributors you invite can see what's inside a
journal. We never make a journal public unless you explicitly turn on
sharing.

### What we do (and don't) do with your data

- We store your memories so you and the people you've invited can
  read them. That's the whole reason Keepswell exists.
- We don't sell your data, ever.
- We don't share your phone number or email with third parties for
  marketing.
- You can delete a journal at any time and we permanently remove the
  memories in it.

For the full details, read our [Privacy Policy](https://keepswell.com/privacy).

## Sharing your memory book

When you want to share what your journal has become — say, at the end
of a year, or for a celebration — Keepswell makes it simple.

- **Public share link** — generate a link that anyone with the link
  can use to view the memory book without signing in. Turn it off
  again any time.
- **Direct invite by phone** — anyone you've added as a contributor
  can view the book anytime through their phone, no account needed.
- **Read together on a big screen** — open the memory book on a
  laptop or TV browser to share around a dinner table.

## Frequently asked questions

**Do my contributors need to download anything?**
No. Anyone can participate over SMS — they just need a phone number
that receives text messages. The iOS app is a nice option for people
who want one, but it's never required.

**Is Keepswell free?**
Yes — you can start a journal and begin capturing memories at no
cost. We offer a Pro plan with extra features like more journals,
more contributors, and SMS prompts. See pricing at
[keepswell.com/pricing](https://keepswell.com/pricing) for current
details.

**Can I have more than one journal?**
Yes. Many people have a few — one for the family, one for each child,
one for a specific trip or event. Each one is independent with its
own prompts and contributors.

**What if a contributor doesn't want to be in the journal anymore?**
They can reply STOP to a text to opt out, and the journal owner can
remove anyone from the participants list at any time.

**Can I print my memory book?**
We're working on print export. Today you can read the full memory
book on the website or in the iOS app, and share the public link with
anyone you want to see it.

**What happens if I delete a journal?**
The journal and every memory, photo, and contributor record inside it
is permanently removed. We can't recover a deleted journal — so we
ask you to type the journal's name to confirm before deleting.

**Can I edit a memory after I share it?**
The person who created a memory can hide or remove it. Owners can
also hide any memory from the memory book.

**Is my data backed up?**
Yes. Memories and photos are stored in secure, replicated cloud
storage. We take backups seriously — your family stories should
outlast any one app or device.

**Can I change a journal's template later?**
Yes. You can switch templates from the journal's settings at any
time. The prompts you've already received and the memories you've
already saved stay exactly as they were.

**Can I turn off prompts?**
Yes — either when you create the journal, or in the settings later.
You can also turn off prompts for just yourself by removing yourself
as a contributor on that journal while keeping the journal active for
others.

**Does Keepswell work outside the US?**
The website and iOS app work worldwide. SMS prompts are currently
optimized for US phone numbers; for non-US numbers we recommend the
in-app or web experience.
